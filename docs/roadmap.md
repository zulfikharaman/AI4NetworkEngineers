# AI for Network Engineers – Learning Roadmap

This roadmap is designed for Network Engineers
(not data scientists or ML engineers).

No heavy math. No unnecessary theory.
Only what is useful in real networking jobs.

---

## Phase 1: AI Basics (For Network Engineers)

- What is AI vs ML vs Generative AI
- What problems AI is good at
- What AI is BAD at (hallucinations, confidence)
- Why network engineers should care

🎯 Goal: Understand AI without fear

## Phase 2: Generative AI & LLMs

- What is an LLM?
- How ChatGPT / Copilot actually works

- Why LLMs hallucinate

🎯 Goal: Know what happens behind the scenes

## Phase 3: Prompt Engineering for Networking

- How to write effective prompts
- Prompting with CLI outputs
- Asking AI to explain configs safely
- Validation-oriented prompts
